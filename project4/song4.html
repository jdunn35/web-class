Reincarnation Apple is one of my favorite PinocchioP songs because I can't pick a single favorite song. It follows a person who's tired of their boring life and buys an apple that allows them to reincarnate, but all of the other lives they reincarnate into have their own problems. It features a lot of references to other PinocchioP songs and has a really well animated music video.
