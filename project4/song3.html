God-ish is PinocchioP's most popular song, with 66 million views - double the amount of his second most popular song. Including covers, it's probably reached over 100 million views. The lyrics play on Japanese slang of comparing things to God, which can be seen with one of the repeating lyrics in the song, "Gott ist tot" which means "God is dead" in German.
