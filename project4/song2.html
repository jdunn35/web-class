NINA is the first PinocchioP song I heard, so it holds a special place in my heart and my playlists. It thinks about a future in which nothing's a mystery anymore and things like flying cars have become a hindrance. In a world like that, can someone invent something that still makes people smile? The fun but introspective lyrics to this song are, in my opinion, what PinocchioP's music is all about.
